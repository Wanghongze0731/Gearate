Humans typically spend a lot of time conceiving, organizing, revising, and optimizing sentences or text content. The AI-generated text is almost instantaneous, which makes people feel that the time to create is compressed and "deprived". In the process of using AI to generate articles, the "process" of creation becomes timeless - only quick results are left. This mode of instant gratification removes the sense of time flow and immersion that people feel during the writing process.
